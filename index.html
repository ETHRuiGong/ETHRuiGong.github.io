Rui Gong  

I am a thrid-year Ph.D. student at the Computer Vision Lab, ETH Zurich, supervised by Prof. Luc Van Gool. Before that, I obtained my master's degree from ETH Zurich, and closely worked with Prof. Wen Li, Dr. Dengxin Dai and Dr. Yuhua Chen. My research interests include computer vision and machine learning, and more specifically in topics such as domain adaptation, transfer learning, image synthesis and scene understanding.
